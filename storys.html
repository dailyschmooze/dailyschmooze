WHO IS OLAMI, AND WHAT IS @DAILYSCHMOOZE:

Olami is a global Jewish organization that is committed to creating a vibrant, committed, and inspired Jewish future. We believe that education and engagement are the keys to creating a strong Jewish identity and countering antisemitism. Our goal is to empower Jewish communities around the world to become leaders in their fields and to inspire others to do the same.
The goal of the social media internship program is to provide the next generation of Jewish leaders with the skills to become online advocates. Olami provides a unique opportunity for college students to gain practical social media skills through hands-on training while contributing to the fight against antisemitism, working closely with staff members to develop strategies and content that engage with audiences and inspire them to take action. The @DailySchmooze social media accounts on Instagram and TikTok were fully conceptualized and developed by our social media interns, who also provide the content which showcases their Jewish heritage and everyday lived experience.
